### Story Failure Points

1. **Motivation drift**: Connect human trafficking → hybrids. Make solving Mélyút crisis part of larger investigation.

2. **Power inconsistency**: Establish clear rules for Jüra's durability. Show damage accumulation. Explain when he's holding back vs. fully engaged.

3. **Scope explosion**: Make Book 1 goal achievable (destroy LOCAL hybrid nest). Set up larger conspiracy for sequels.

4. **Deus ex machina**: Add setup, cost, or trade-offs to convenient solutions. Make victories earned.

5. **Underdeveloped romance**: Add 2-3 intimate scenes exploring how transformation affects their relationship. Create relationship conflict around Jüra's leash/uncertain future.

6. **Information overload**: Consolidate mysteries. One revelation should answer multiple questions. Space reveals across books.
